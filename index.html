QUESTIONNAIRE
ON THE
HISTORICAL AND CULTURAL SIGNIFICANCE
OF PAVIA'S FORTIFICATIONS.

A Master's Thesis Research By Obafemi Yusuf. O.
University of Pavia

Be a part of this project!!

Thank you for participating in this Academic research! This questionnaire is part of a study on the Historical evolution, preservation, and cultural relevance of Pavias defensive walls and gates.

The collected data will contribute to a deeper understanding of public perceptions, preservation standards, and the potential for urban regeneration and tourism development linked to these historic structures.

The questionnaire should take approximately 3-4 minutes to complete.

DISCLAIMER!! : Your responses are invaluable, and they will be used strictly for academic purposes ONLY. Your personal data will not be collected, and all information provided will remain confidential and anonymous. This disclaimer is in compliance with GDPR EU 2016/679.
